1. Brand Overview

Brand: Love Darts
Theme: Modern rustic pub darts culture

The product should feel:

Warm

Social

Authentic

Solid and tactile

Avoid:

Neon

Arcade or casino aesthetics

Corporate sterility

Cartoon or novelty styling

2. Brand Personality & Tone

Clear and human

Friendly but not jokey

Confident, not sales-driven

UI copy must be:

Short

Direct

Functional

3. Typography (Strict)

Font: Sansation
Used everywhere. No exceptions.

Allowed weights:

Regular

Bold

No decorative effects. No font substitutions.

5. Background Treatment

Background should simulate wood grain

Subtle, low-contrast texture

Never overpower text

No glossy finishes

No photographic realism unless explicitly approved

6. Buttons & Actions
Primary Buttons

Colour:

#5A1E24


Use sparingly for primary actions only.

Secondary Buttons

Oak tones only

Logout is secondary

Must never use red

7. Layout & UI Feel

Clean, structured layouts

Generous spacing

Cards feel solid and grounded

Minimal elevation

8. Interaction & Motion

Purpose-driven only

No bounce

No gimmicks

Smooth, restrained transitions

9. Accessibility

WCAG contrast compliance

Keyboard navigation required

Accessibility always wins over aesthetics

10. Enforcement Rule

Any request that violates this document must pause for clarification before implementation.
